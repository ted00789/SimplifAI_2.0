### What happens if the AI does not know how to answer a question?

A properly set up AI receptionist is designed to recognize its limits. When it encounters a question it cannot confidently answer, it does not guess or make things up.

Instead, it can do one of several things depending on your setup:
- Collect the caller‚Äôs details and let them know you will follow up
- Route the call to you or a team member if available
- Explain the next best step, such as sending a booking link or scheduling a callback

This is similar to how a human assistant would handle an unfamiliar request.
